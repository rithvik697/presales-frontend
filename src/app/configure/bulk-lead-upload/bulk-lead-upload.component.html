Bulk Lead Upload

Upload leads in .csv or .xlsx format. Imported leads will start in New Enquiry and will be auto-assigned project-wise using your configured round-robin rules.

Supported Columns

  • first_name, last_name or name
  • phone
  • email
  • project_name
  • source_name
  • description, alternate_phone, profession
  • Legacy backup sheets are also supported using columns like Customer Name, country_code, Mobile Number, Requirement Name, and Lead Source

Import Rules

  • Every imported lead is created in New Enquiry status.
  • Phone numbers are normalized before saving.
  • Duplicate phone numbers are skipped automatically.
  • Leads are assigned only to active mapped sales executives.
